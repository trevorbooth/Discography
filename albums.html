Released in 1993, this is the first TOOL full-length studio album. This album helped "heavy-metal" and "progressive rock" push through to the mainstream. As of 2010, the album has sold 2,910,000 copies and was considered a double platinum by RIAA (Recording Industry Association of America). Although, the album was removed from the shelf temporarily after Walmart & Kmart had a problem with some of the photos in the linear notes of the album.
