Biography

TOOL surfaced in the early 90's, TOOL brought a new genre of music to the mainstream. Pioneering a heavy metal genre and making TOOL a house hold name in the 90's. The band formed in L.A. and came up with the name "TOOLSHED". The name was later shorten to just TOOL. Within a year of coming together they signed with a record company, Zoo Entertainment. 1992, they released an EP album and then in 93' "Undertow" was released. This was there first studio album, and from this point was the start.

Maynard James Keenan. Lead vocalist for TOOL, you think this would be his biggest accoplishment, but there is way more to Keenan. Keenan was a poet in High School & he was also a Cross-Country champion. After HS Keanan joined the army and would become a student a Westpoint for a short time. After the army, he moved to L.A. to pursue interior design. This is where TOOL was formed. Along with TOOL, Keenan is also the lead vocalist for "A Perfect Circle" and "Puscifer". He currently owns his own brand of wine located in California. Keenan also has his purple belt in Bjj, which took him approximatly 6 years to get. He has quite a life; a biography called "A Perfect Union of Contrary Things" has just released. You can know read all about his experiences.
